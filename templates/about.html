About Laptop Price Predictor

How It Works

The Laptop Price Predictor uses machine learning to estimate the price of a laptop based on its specifications. Our model was trained on thousands of laptop configurations to provide accurate price predictions.

Features Considered
  • Brand and model type
  • RAM capacity
  • Storage type and capacity (HDD/SSD)
  • Display features (Touchscreen, IPS, PPI)
  • Processor and GPU brand
  • Operating system
  • Weight
Note

These predictions are estimates based on historical data and market trends. Actual prices may vary based on region, availability, and other market factors.
